香道师
详情

将香、花、茶融入到生活美学中。还原生活回归本心

木之森首席香道师，制香师，中国传统香学倡导者，跟随多位香学导师、中医名师悟道学香，立志于香与生活的链接。主张中国传统素养文化，将香、花、插融入到生活美学中， 还原生活的简单与纯粹，回归本心.

Muzhisen Chief Xiangdao, a perfumer, a traditional Chinese aroma advocate, followed a number of fragrant tutors, famous Chinese doctors, and learned the link between incense and life. Advocating Chinese traditional literacy culture, integrating incense, flower, and insertion into the aesthetics of life, restoring the simplicity and purity of life, returning to the heart.
